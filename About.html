About Cyber Vigilan-Tem
Hello! I'm a passionate cybersecurity student right here at the Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO) and the founder of CYBER VIGILAN-TEM & CYBER STACK .
Like many of you, I was diving deep into the world of cybersecurity and found myself searching all over the internet for the best learning materials.
A significant part of that challenge was navigating the scattered landscape of online tutorials, labs, and articles. I realized that if I was looking for structured resources, my fellow students in SICT probably were too.
That’s why I built this website. My goal was to create a simple, organized, and central place for all of us—whether you're in the Cyber Security department, CSC, IFT, or just curious—to find quality resources without the hassle.

OUR MISSION & VISION:

The vision for Cyber Vigilan-Tem is to be a dedicated hub designed to support fellow students and the world at large.
This site is more than just a collection of links; it’s a starting point for aspiring security analysts, penetration testers, and digital forensics investigators from FUTO to build a strong foundation.
This platform is my way of contributing to our shared learning journey. It's built for the FUTO community, by a member of the community, with the belief that knowledge should be accessible to everyone.
This project is open-source, and contributions are always welcome to help our community grow. I hope it helps you on your path to becoming a cybersecurity professional.
Let's remain vigilant and build a secure digital future together.
Happy Hacking!
